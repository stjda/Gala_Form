STJDA Annual Gala: A Night of Hope and Celebration

Join us for an unforgettable evening at the South Texas Juvenile Diabetes Association's Annual Gala. This year's theme, "Empowering Dreams, Transforming Lives," celebrates the resilience of children living with diabetes and the impact of our community's support.

Our gala is not just an event; it's a powerful opportunity to make a lasting difference in the lives of children with diabetes. Your attendance and generous contributions will directly support our year-round programs, including educational workshops, summer camps, and cutting-edge research initiatives.
